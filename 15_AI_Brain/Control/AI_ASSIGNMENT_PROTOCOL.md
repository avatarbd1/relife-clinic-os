# AI ASSIGNMENT PROTOCOL

Purpose:
Define how AI workers receive and manage tasks.

## Assignment Flow

Owner Goal
↓
Brain Analysis
↓
Task Created
↓
AI Registry Check
↓
AI Assigned
↓
Task Queue Lock
↓
Work
↓
Handover Update
↓
Review
↓
Done

## Rules

1. Every AI must have an ID from AI_REGISTRY.md.

2. One AI can hold one active task at a time.

3. Same file/module cannot be worked on by two AI workers simultaneously.

4. Production files require required approval before changes.

5. Every completed task must update:
- TASK_QUEUE.md
- HANDOVER.md
