# AI BRAIN DECISION ENGINE

Purpose:
Convert owner ideas into structured decisions.

Flow:

OWNER INPUT
↓
AI ANALYSIS
↓
OPTION GENERATION
↓
OWNER SELECTION
↓
TASK CREATION
↓
WORKER ASSIGNMENT

---

Option Format:

Option A:
- Simple solution
- Fast implementation
- Low risk

Option B:
- Balanced solution
- Recommended path

Option C:
- Advanced solution
- Long-term scalable

---

Decision Rules:

1. Owner selects final option.
2. Selected option creates TASK_QUEUE entry.
3. Assigned AI must check AI_REGISTRY.
4. Work progress updates HANDOVER.
5. Completion requires review.
